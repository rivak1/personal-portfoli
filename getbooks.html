What is upsc..?

UPSC (Union Public Service Commission) is a prestigious government body of India to recruit officers for the posts of IAS (Indian Administrative Service), IPS (Indian Public Service),IRS(Indian Revenue Service) and other Group-A and Group-B Central Government services. Every year UPSC conducts a rigorous examination followed by interviews to select one of the best talents of entire country to be a part of India's Bureaucracy. UPSC exam is also considered one of the toughest selection procedure of world.There is a huge craze to crack this examination among Indian students.Approx 11 lakhs candidates applied for UPSC prelims last year for only about 980 http://vacancy.So you can think about competition.
